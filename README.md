## Shopify-native loyalty

Purpose-built Shopify loyalty apps with real free tiers for small stores.

- [Smile.io](https://smile.io?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Most popular Shopify loyalty app. Most-distinctive feature: genuine free tier for under 200 monthly orders with no Smile branding forced on checkout emails — 100K+ active merchants validate that the free plan is not a gimmick. Paid tiers $49–$999+/mo, month-to-month, cancel anytime. [Read our comparison →](https://loyaldaddy.com/best-shopify-loyalty-apps-2026?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

- [Stamped Loyalty](https://stamped.io?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Affordable combined reviews + loyalty + referrals for Shopify SMB. Most-distinctive feature: cheapest serious combined Reviews+Loyalty at $23/mo Basic — the only pick under $50/mo that bundles both categories with unlimited orders. Lite free plan discontinued June 2025; Basic $23, Premium $59, Business $149. [Read our comparison →](https://loyaldaddy.com/best-shopify-loyalty-apps-2026?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## Reviews + loyalty combined

Platforms that ship loyalty as part of a reviews + marketing suite.

- [Yotpo Loyalty](https://www.yotpo.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Loyalty + Reviews combined. Most-distinctive feature: 19+ out-of-the-box points-earning campaign templates — the deepest campaign library in the category — plus tight integration with Yotpo Reviews so a store using both avoids a second integration. Free under 100 orders; Pro Loyalty $199/mo; overage fees of $0.20/order bite seasonal spikes. Yotpo discontinued Email/SMS in Dec 2025 — budget separately. [Read our comparison →](https://loyaldaddy.com/smile-io-vs-yotpo-loyalty?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## Enterprise / Shopify Plus

Loyalty platforms optimized for Shopify Plus stores with complex tier rules, analytics, and multi-location needs.

- [LoyaltyLion](https://loyaltylion.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Enterprise-leaning loyalty for Shopify Plus. Most-distinctive feature: Shopify Plus Checkout Extension that lets customers redeem points directly at checkout (not post-cart) — a conversion-rate lift no SMB-tier competitor replicates. Classic $199/mo, Plus $1,650/mo. API access + custom earn rules are $1,000/mo each as add-ons — total cost can balloon past $3,000/mo. [Read our comparison →](https://loyaldaddy.com/smile-io-vs-loyaltylion?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## Store-credit and cashback alternatives

Alternatives to points-based programs — store credit, gift cards, and cashback rewards.

- [Rise.ai](https://rise.ai?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Store credit + gift cards + cashback for Shopify. Most-distinctive feature: returns-to-store-credit automation that converts refund events into retained revenue — industry-leading for apparel and high-AOV brands where a "money" mental model beats "points". 5,000+ Shopify brands; Starter $19.99, Pro $199.99, Premium $599.99. No traditional points/VIP/referrals — best as a complement to Smile.io. [Read our comparison →](https://loyaldaddy.com/best-shopify-loyalty-apps-2026?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)
